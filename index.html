About WizWare Technologies Inc.

WizWare Technologies is an IT company providing a wide variety of services. From website hosting and networking to collaborating on open-source projects such as the HumHub social platform, WizWare Technologies offers creates solutions all over the world of IT.


What did I do?

My primary project at WizWare has been to build a module or plugin for an open-source community based social media platform called HumHub. This required me to work with a variety of languages and systems such as PHP, HTML, CSS, SQL, and the Yii2 framework. While this project is still a work in process, my time at WizWare also saw me doing a variety of other things, such as using a CNC machine to make computer designs a reality and building simple electronics by soldering.
